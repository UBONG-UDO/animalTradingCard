Rabbit

Rabbit are small mammals in the family Leporidae, which is in the order Lagomorpha. They are familiar throughout the world as a small herbivore, a prey animal, a domesticated form of livestock, and a pet, having a widespread effect on ecologies and culture.


Basic Facts About Rabbit:


  • Scientific Name: Oryctolagus cuniculus (domestic)
  • Lifespan: 7 to 12 years
  • Diet: Herbivorous
  • Habitat: Grasslands, Forests, meadows
  • Reproduction: Very fast breeders
